NIBM

The National Institute of Business Management (NIBM) is a leading education institute in Sri Lanka in the fields of Management and IT. It was established by the government of Sri Lanka in 1968 and has since grown to become one of the largest and most prestigious educational institutions in the country. NIBM offers a wide range of courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, including diplomas, degrees, and professional qualifications.
